Upcoming webinar

Join us for a free webinar on Thursday, Sept. 28, from 2–3 p.m. ET, to learn about disaster preparedness. When a catastrophe like Hurricane Harvey happens, it may be difficult for people to know where to look for guidance and assistance, as well as what financial steps to take as they begin recovering. Even if your area isn’t affected this time, you may want to help people prepare—for upcoming catastrophes or just for their peace of mind. On this webinar, we will discuss tools and resources available from the CFPB and other federal agencies to help consumers prepare for and cope with the financial aspects of disasters.

Email CFPB_FinEx@consumerfinance.gov to reserve your spot.
